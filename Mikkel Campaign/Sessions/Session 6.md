## Session notes
We start in the loggings the Equjay provide for us. 

Willow and Cerris have a conversation about tactics in fights. 

After some discussions, we move into the jungle to find the first totem. 

At the totem we try shooting at it, both with gun and magic, but after failing to do so we get attacked by a fire breathing monkey. 

We fight the monkey. and win 

we then decide to attack the totem, rushing in and first Cerris gets petrified, then Darwin gets stunned but Darwin made it up to the totem and ripped it from the ground. Darwin then popped the gems from the sockets in the Totem. When he picked up the gems, he saw a vision of the Great dragon Dahak and dropped the gems. Then Darwin did the same but trying to pick them up in a bag, but saw the same vision and dropped the gems too. Cerris then picked up the gems and the gems burned and shattered.

We then picked up the totem and returned to the village of the Equjay. 

In the Village of the Equjay the celebrate us bringing the Totem back and hold a feast for us. 

Darwin, learns alot of basic information about the Equjay, food, crafts, imports/exports we have now access to the Mwangi market

At the feast we see alot of different races gather and feel the moral is rising and hope is returning to these people. 

Cerris and Willow have a conversation, where Willow is asking Cerris why he is resistant to magic. Cerris tries to tell Willow but, a devil takes over Cerris and teach Cerris a lesson, but it ends up with showing Willow that there is something sometimes controlling Cerris.

Darwin returns to the room as Willow is leaving, and then questions Cerris more. After Willow returns one of the gods that have interest in Cerris returns to answer some questions. 

We rest, and then the next day we return to the second totem. 

At the second totem we prepare as well as we think we could. turn Cerris invisible and sneak up to the totem at 120ft. but we all start seeing vision 

Willow saw visions in the under dark where he was working in the slave gang and his "caregiver" got her head chopped of. 

Cerris saw his body being torned apart by two gods by pulling on his hands. 

Darwin saw Voz who tried to convince him about the lies about the founder of Breach hill was a slaver. 

After resisting the mental effects then Darwin woke up in the Jungle and realize we where affected by the totem. So he rushed up to the totem and popped the gems out with his dagger. 

we gathered up again and shared what we saw and tried to calm down. 

Renali then sendt out some spiders to search for the third totem, and we returned to the Equjay town.
