## Session notes

We start at the cabin, with the three NPC, Anna, Clem and Darrion. 

We discussed what to do next and decided to move towards Telvir, because south of Telvir Timothys parents live and he wanted to warn them about the invasion of the citadel and maybe send a bird to warn Franks family living north of the Fellmans peaks. 

 we start moving towards the middle of Boars wood, since there is a river there where we can either split up, going to Franks Family or Timothies, or we continue on to one side and send a bird to the other.

First area we arrive at there is an Altar to Serenre. 
Next area we find some camp fire that was recently used, and decide to stay here.  The best Ruen know this area was a town/keep from before the boars wood grew, This was most likely Trakkens keep.

we set up watches for the night, 

the next day we move south towards where there might have been a marketplace. there we find 4 udefhans camped there by a well roasting a woman on a spit. After some discussions, we decide not to attack them as this feels like a trap and double back and then move south east

we move south east and find an old abandoned logging camp. some of the houses are very derelict but others seem like they are well kept. Ruen walked up to one of the more well kept houses and opened the door. Inside he found a disgusting scene where humans and goblins had been slaughtered, seemed like the humans had attacked the goblins. 

We meet goblins, Frank can understand them so he communicates with them. We find out they are Froblins, we make a deal with them trading 5 rations for some water. Ruen finds a well behind one of the houses and trying to push a goblin away, he pours water over him and the goblin tries setting him on fire. 

Frank, tells the goblins that they cant set the houses on fire since the fire is tired. then the goblins went away. Ruen tells the group of the well and they gather as much water they can from the well, and then notice a something just under the water. Timothy goes into the water and followes a path through the hole in the well into a cave, where he sees something coming from the darkness and he jumps back into the water and gets pulled back up. 

Following Frank from well, comes a small water elemental, that comes out of the well an floats into one of the houses and envelopes one of the dead men there, then returns to the well after "sniffing" at Ruen. 

We then try to find out what happened at the logging camp, in the slaughter scene, we have few dead goblins and 2 humans. A human woman, is clutching a goblin child, who both seem to have died from some kind of decease as they are covered in brown bile. The man is laying infront of them trying to reach them, but the rest of them are riddled by stabbing wounds. 

In another house we find some dead goblins and a man, who by his aparell looks like he was somekind of a wizard/witch/medicine man. 

Ruen and Timothy examine the medicine man more. on the medicine man we find a talisman of lovers knot, 

Clem and Frank examine the dead goblin child. and found a rotten potato in his mouth, making us think all the food was poisoned.
